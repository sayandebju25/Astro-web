FAQs About Astrology

What is Kundali?

Kundali (also known as Janampatri or Birth Chart) is an astrological chart created based on your date, time, and place of birth. It provides insights into various aspects of your life.

What is Horoscope Reading?

Horoscope reading involves analyzing the position of planets and stars at the time of your birth to provide predictions and guidance for your life.

How does Kundli Matching work?

Kundli Matching compares the birth charts of two individuals to check for compatibility in marriage, ensuring a harmonious life together.

Is astrology accurate?

Astrology provides valuable insights into personality traits, life challenges, and potential outcomes based on celestial positions. It has helped millions of people, but accuracy depends on various factors.
